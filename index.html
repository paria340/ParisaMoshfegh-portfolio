ABOUT ME

Greetings! I am a seasoned Frontend Software Developer with a track record of success at OwnersBucks and Bonjour-Residence. At OwnersBucks, I played a pivotal role in building engaging game features using Vue.js on the frontend and Java on the backend. This involved creating dynamic user experiences, including lightning lineups, battle passes, and salary cap management. My expertise extended to implementing Jest unit testing and conducting thorough cucumber testing to ensure the reliability and stability of these features.

Beyond development, I showcased strong leadership skills by managing a marketing platform project from start to finish. Thriving in a fast-paced agile environment, I excelled in erasing tech debt and contributing to the overall efficiency of the development process. My multifaceted experience and commitment to excellence make me a valuable asset for innovative software development projects.
